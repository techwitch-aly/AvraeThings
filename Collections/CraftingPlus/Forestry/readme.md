__**FORESTRY CHECKS**__

Craftable items will require a variety of materials, many of which come from nature. To obtain wood materials, players will utilize the command `!forestry`. This command is based off of a survival roll and will give you different items depending on the outcome of your roll. These materials can be found at <#1216894924012654765> in Elysia, <#1190198610437083226> on Emberisle, and <#1169493746174394428> in Skalavik.

* 5 or below (or a nat1 regardless of modifiers) - firewood
* 6 to 10 - two wooden branches
* 11 to 15 - one quality branch
* 16 to 20 - two wooden branches and one quality branch
* Over 20 - three wooden branches and one quality branch
